Job Title: Psychological Research Assistant

Job Summary:


The Psychological Research Assistant supports psychological research projects through active participation in designing studies, collecting and analyzing data, and maintaining research documentation. The assistant will ensure ethical compliance, maintain research integrity, and contribute to publications and presentations. This role is ideal for individuals passionate about behavioral research and pursuing a career in psychology or related fields.

Key Duties and Responsibilities:


1. Research Support:


o Assist in the development of research instruments (e.g., surveys, experiments).

o Conduct comprehensive literature reviews.

o Prepare and edit research proposals, presentations, and academic papers.

2. Data Collection and Participant Handling:


o Recruit and communicate with study participants.

o Administer psychological assessments and surveys in accordance with study protocols.

o Maintain accurate and confidential records of participant data.

3. Data Management and Analysis:


o Input, clean, and organize data using statistical software (e.g., SPSS, R).

o Perform basic statistical analyses and support data interpretation.

o Generate visual and written summaries of findings.

Required Qualifications:


• Educational Qualification: 3rd year or above in Psychology or a relevant discipline from a well-reputed university with a minimum CGPA of 3.20.

• Familiarity with psychological research methodologies and data analysis techniques.

• Basic computer skill (familiarity with SPSS or R will be an advantage).

• Proficient in academic writing and communication.

Preferred Qualifications:


• Prior experience in research assistantship or academic research.

• Experience with qualitative data analysis is a plus.

• APA formatting knowledge.

Key Skills and Competencies:


• Attention to detail and data accuracy.

• Analytical thinking and problem-solving ability.

• Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

• Ability to maintain confidentiality and professionalism.

Working Conditions:


• Based in Chittagong, working within office and laboratory settings.

• May require flexible hours depending on research timelines.

• Involves significant time working on computers and interacting with participants.

Performance Criteria:


• Precision and reliability in data handling.

• Timely completion of research-related tasks.

• Quality of research outputs and contribution to publications.

• Compliance with ethical and institutional standards.
